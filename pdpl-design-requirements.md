## 11 Golden Rules:
1. No element without animation
2. No card without Glassmorphism
3. No number without counter
4. No page without ParticleField
5. No transition without animation
6. No button without glow
7. No theme without integration (dark+light)
8. No list without collapse
9. No modal without blur
10. No interaction without sound
11. No element without RTL
